Our Mission

The University of the Assumption commits itself to the integral development of Catholic leaders through Academic Excellence, Christian Formation, and Community Services.

Our Vision

The University of the Assumption an Archdiocesan Catholic educational institution envisions itself as the leading Formator of academically competent, morally upright, and socially responsible Catholic leaders.

University Goals

To fulfill its mission, the University of the Assumption pursues the following objectives:

Witnessing Gospel Values

Developing students who live out their faith through prayer, liturgy, and community service.

Educational Milieu

Creating an environment that fosters intellectual inquiry, reflective judgment, and resolute action in the arts and sciences.

Social Transformation

Encouraging a strong sense of responsibility so members can participate in the growth of families and nations.

Professional Leadership

Continuously providing society with ethical professionals for technological and economic progress.

Core Values

The university community is guided by seven core values:

Servant Leadership

Prioritizing service to others and the poor.

Integrity

Ensuring words and actions are in accord, guided by the spiritual integrity of Mary.

Excellence

Delivering exemplary outcomes through hard work and constant improvement.

Service Orientation

Caring for the environment and providing service to stakeholders.

Teamwork

Building relationships based on collaboration, cooperation, and complementation.

Obedience

Using humility and adherence to rules as guides for the will of God.

Open Communication

Valuing the free exchange of ideas, dialogue, and constructive feedback.
