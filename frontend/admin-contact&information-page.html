General Policy on Parking Lot Use

In line with our commitment to safety, order, and the fair use of campus facilities, the University of the Assumption is implementing the following guidelines for the use of its parking areas. All members of the University community, including staff, students, and guests, are expected to comply with these regulations:

1. Authorized Use

Only vehicles with valid authorization may park within the University premises. These include:

  • School personnel with approved parking privileges and a valid University-issued parking sticker.
  • Enrolled students with registered parking permits and visible vehicle stickers.
  • Official visitors with a temporary visitor's pass issued by the security personnel at the entrance.

2. Staff Parking

Designated parking zones are assigned to faculty and staff. A valid University parking sticker must be clearly displayed at all times. Unauthorized use of staff parking spaces is not permitted.

3. Visitor Parking

All visitors are required to:

  • Report to the security office at the entrance upon arrival.
  • Present a valid government-issued ID in exchange for a Visitor's Parking Pass.
  • Park only in the designated visitor parking area.
  • Return the parking pass to security personnel to reclaim their ID upon exit.

4. Parking Rules and Restrictions

All drivers must comply with posted signs, designated parking zones, and security personnel instructions. Parking in restricted areas (e.g., fire lanes, driveways, reserved slots) is strictly prohibited.

5. Enforcement and Violations

Violations of parking rules may result in the following:

  • First Offense: Written warning or reprimand.
  • Second Offense: Revocation of parking privileges.
  • Severe Cases: Towing of the vehicle at the owner's expense.

6. Liability Disclaimer

The University assumes no responsibility for loss, theft, or damage to vehicles or their contents while parked on the parking facility. Drivers are strongly advised to secure their vehicles and avoid leaving valuables inside.

7. Common Parking Violations

Below is a list of common violations that may result in warnings, penalties, or revocation of parking privileges within the school parking facility:

  • Parking without a valid permit or payment.
  • Displaying an expired, invalid, forged, or copied permit.
  • Using a forged, stolen, or copied permit.
  • Occupying more than one parking space.
  • Parking in a reserved or restricted space (e.g., for staff, visitors, PWDs, or EVs).
  • Blocking driveways, fire lanes, emergency exits, or pedestrian paths.
  • Parking in "No Parking" zones.
  • Obstructing another vehicle.
  • Parking in unauthorized areas (e.g., staff or faculty area).
  • Leaving abandoned or inoperable vehicles in the lot.
  • Parking oversized or unauthorized vehicles (e.g., trucks, trailers).
  • Idling.
